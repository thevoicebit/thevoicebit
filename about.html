ABOUT US

VISION

An initiation of the NGO Durga, The Voice-BIT envisions to address all the cutting edge social issues, keeping women safety and women empowerment major focal issues. Through service, advocacy and friendship we envision to create a utopian society.’The great hope of society is in individual character’ and thus creating an environment,conducive to help individuals develop a dutiful responsive attitude is our aspiration.

1. To expand women’s access to justice by improving psychological well-being of women.
2. To provide technical assistance to prevent gender based violence.
3. To inspire women reach the zenith of their potential by organizing events, workshops and other activities that will help them connect, support and recognize one another.
